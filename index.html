The Resurrection Story

Palm Sunday

Jesus enters Jerusalem riding on a donkey as crowds welcome Him with palm branches, fulfilling the prophecy of the coming Messiah.

Last Supper

Jesus shares His final Passover meal with His disciples, establishing the sacrament of communion and preparing them for what is to come.

Garden of Gethsemane

Jesus prays intensely before His arrest, showing His humanity and submission to God's will despite the suffering ahead.

Crucifixion

Jesus is crucified at Golgotha, taking upon Himself the sins of humanity and fulfilling His purpose as the ultimate sacrifice.

The Resurrection

On the third day, Jesus rises from the dead, conquering death and sin, bringing hope and the promise of eternal life to all believers.

Great Commission

The risen Christ appears to His disciples and commissions them to share the good news of salvation throughout the world.

The Meaning of Easter

Easter celebrates the resurrection of Jesus Christ from the dead, which is the foundation of Christian faith. After His crucifixion and burial, Jesus rose from the dead on the third day, conquering sin and death.

This miraculous event offers hope, redemption, and the promise of eternal life to all who believe. It reminds us that through faith in Christ, we too can experience new life and spiritual transformation.
